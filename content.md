#### A Note from the Founder

![Founder Image](https://framerusercontent.com/images/Bnr34xpvgZdufcsLfEjVbwzTFWU.jpg?width=1961&height=2248)

Dear friends,

At 3 Circles, we’ve always believed in building more than just websites—we build connections, experiences, and trust. As we reflect on our path so far, it’s clear that growth is never linear. It comes with highs, lows, and lessons that shape who we are today.

2023 was a challenging year for us. We had to make some tough decisions, scale back operations, and rethink our priorities.

Looking ahead, our vision for 2025 is simple yet ambitious: refine our craft, strengthen our brand, and continue growing sustainably.

"Growth isn’t a straight line — it’s the courage to rebuild, refine, and rise stronger than before, turning every challenge into a stepping stone toward a future we’re proud to create.”

With gratitude,

#### Michael rey

Founder, Three Circles
